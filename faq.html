Frequently asked questions

Quick answers for posters and taskers across Canada

What is QuickGigs? +
QuickGigs is a Canada-wide marketplace where you post everyday tasks or earn money completing them — errands, home help, tutoring, beauty, tech, and more.
Is QuickGigs live everywhere in Canada? +
Yes. We're live across Canada during beta. Post and browse tasks in your city — we're expanding to more countries soon.
How do payments work? +
Beta mode: payments are not live yet. You can post, apply, message after accept, and complete tasks. Stripe escrow launches before public release.
When can I message someone? +
Chat unlocks when a poster accepts your application (beta). This keeps conversations tied to real jobs.
Do taskers need a profile photo? +
Yes. Taskers must upload a clear photo before applying so posters know who they're hiring.
What if something goes wrong? +
Use Report on a task or profile, or open a Dispute from My Tasks. See our dispute process. Email support@quickgigs.ca.
How long do tasks stay open? +
Open tasks automatically expire after 30 days with no hire. Post again anytime.
How do I get notified? +
Enable notifications on your dashboard for messages and accepted jobs. Email alerts send to your signup address when configured.
Who can sign up? +
You must be 18 or older and agree to our Terms and Privacy Policy.
