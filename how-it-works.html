How QuickGigs works

Three steps — for posters and taskers across Canada

1

Post or browse

Posters describe what they need, set a budget, and pick a category. Taskers browse open tasks near them and apply with an offer and message.

2

Get matched

Posters review applicants and accept the best fit. Taskers get notified when hired. Chat opens after accept so you can coordinate details safely on-platform.

3

Task gets done

Work happens, both sides mark complete, and leave reviews. Disputes and reports are available if something goes wrong.

Two modes: Quick for urgent help, Standard to plan ahead and compare applicants. Beta: payments coming soon — explore the full flow now.
