Dispute resolution

What happens when something goes wrong

Beta note: Payments are not live yet. Disputes are logged and reviewed by our team. When Stripe escrow launches, payment holds and releases will follow these same steps.

1

Tell us what happened

From My Tasks → In progress or Completed, tap Dispute. Describe the issue and keep messages on QuickGigs when possible — we review chat history.

2

We review within 48 hours

Our team reads the task details, applications, chat, and photos. We may email both parties for more information.

3

Outcome

Depending on the case we may: confirm the job as complete, cancel the task, warn or ban an account, or (when payments are live) release or refund escrow. Both parties receive an email summary.

Before opening a dispute
  • Try to resolve it in chat first — many issues are misunderstandings.
  • Use Report for safety issues (harassment, scams, inappropriate behaviour).
  • Use Dispute for job quality, no-shows, or payment disagreements.
