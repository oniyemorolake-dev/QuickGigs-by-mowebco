Trust & safety

Stay safe on QuickGigs

Meeting new people for tasks — protect yourself

QuickGigs blocks phone numbers and emails in chat to reduce off-platform scams. Keep communication on the platform until you trust the other person.

Before you meet
  • Review profiles, photos, ratings, and trust badges before hiring or accepting.
  • Agree on scope, price, and timing in chat before starting work.
  • Meet in public or well-lit areas when possible for first jobs.
  • Tell a friend or family member where you're going and when you'll be back.
  • Never share banking passwords, SIN, or ID photos in chat.
During the task
  • Trust your instincts — leave if something feels wrong.
  • Don't handle large cash amounts off-platform during beta.
  • Take reference photos of work when appropriate (posters can request updates).
If something feels off
  • Tap Report on a task or profile immediately.
  • Open a Dispute from My Tasks if a job went badly.
  • Email support@quickgigs.ca for urgent issues.
  • Call 911 in an emergency — QuickGigs support is not emergency services.
