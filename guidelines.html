Guidelines

What we expect from posters and taskers

Writing a good task listing
  • Use a clear title — what needs doing, not just "help needed".
  • Describe scope, access, tools provided, and timing.
  • Set a fair budget (minimum $20 on QuickGigs).
  • Add your city/region so taskers know where the job is.
  • Upload reference photos when it helps (damage, layout, items).
  • Respond to applicants within a reasonable time.
Not allowed
  • Illegal work, adult services, or unsafe requests.
  • Discrimination based on protected grounds.
  • Asking taskers to work off-platform to avoid fees (when payments launch).
  • Harassment, threats, or abusive messages.
Being a great tasker
  • Keep your profile photo, bio, skills, and service area up to date.
  • Only apply to tasks you can actually complete on time.
  • Write a personal application — explain why you're a fit.
  • Arrive on time, communicate delays early, and do quality work.
  • Mark jobs complete honestly and leave fair reviews.
Not allowed
  • No-shows, fake profiles, or misleading skills.
  • Sharing contact info to bypass QuickGigs chat rules.
  • Demanding payment outside the platform (when escrow is live).
  • Subcontracting without the poster's knowledge unless agreed.

Violations may lead to warnings, suspension, or a permanent ban. See Terms of Service.
